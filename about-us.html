Dr. Pawel Zimny

A few words about us

The founder of the 'Infinita Perfectio' clinic is Dr. Pawel. Pawel graduated from the Faculty of Dentistry at the Medical University of Lodz in 1996. In 1999, he completed a specialisation in general dentistry, and in 2005, he passed the specialisation exam, obtaining the title of specialist in dental prosthetics. In 2010, Dr. Pawel completed postgraduate studies at New York University in the field of dental implantology. In the same year, the Nationwide Association of Dentistry OSIS-EDI awarded Dr. Pawel a certificate of skills in the field of dental implantology, granted by the Board for Skills. Dr. Pawel is a leader in his field, establishing workplaces and also training other doctors. For over 20 years, Dr. Pawel worked as a dentist in the Polish Army and at the Dental Outpatient Clinic. In 2018, Dr. Pawel opened a private clinic with the most demanding patients in mind, seeking innovative and painless solutions in dental treatment. The 'Infinita Perfectio' offices are equipped with the highest quality equipment, ensuring comfort and the effectiveness of treatment. The most experienced team of doctors works here, regularly participating in numerous trainings both in the country and abroad. Each dentist in our team adheres to the highest quality standards based on experience, continuous competence improvement, and caring for the well-being of the patient.
